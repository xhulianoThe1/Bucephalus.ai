Welcome to the official site for the University at Albany School of Business Investment Group (UASBIG).

The University at Albany School of Business Investment Group is a premier undergraduate organization founded in 2008 that seeks to prepare students for personal and professional success in investing. Managing a portfolio of nearly $300,000 in diversified assets, UASBIG is one of the largest undergraduate funds in the nation.
